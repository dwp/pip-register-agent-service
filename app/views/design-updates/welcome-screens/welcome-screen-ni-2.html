After this call we will send you a form called ‘How your disability affects you’

This is where you tell us about your conditions or disabilities and how they affect your life. We will also ask you to send us any more information to support your claim.

An independent health professional will look at all the information you give us

You may need to have an assessment.

It is important you attend this assessment. Otherwise, the Department for Communities may stop your claim.

You can ask the health assessor to record your assessment. To do this, please contact your assessor when you receive your appointment. After your appointment you will receive a link to your recording by text message or email.

If you have any questions about your PIP claim after this call

You can contact us by phone. You can also call us to report any changes to:

  • your address
  • phone number
  • account details, or
  • your health condition.

Do you have a way of noting down the phone number?

The phone number is 0800 121 4433.

You may like to save this number to your phone contacts.
