Do you have a mental health condition or a condition that causes you significant difficulties remembering things, learning new things, or making everyday decisions?

  • Conduct disorder
  • Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD)
  • Down syndrome
  • developmental delay
  • Alzheimer’s or Dementia
